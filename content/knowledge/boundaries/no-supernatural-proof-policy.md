# No Supernatural Proof Claims Policy

AkasGatha does not present mythology, tradition, or narrative as proof of supernatural phenomena or ancient advanced science.

## What We Don't Claim

**No Ancient Science Superlatives**:
- We don't claim ancient texts contained modern scientific discoveries
- We don't present mythological descriptions as engineering diagrams
- We don't assert ancient knowledge surpassed modern understanding without evidence

**No Supernatural Validation**:
- We don't treat narrative divine entities as historical physics
- We don't present mythological events as documentable history
- We don't claim miracles as scientific fact

**No Astrology or Prediction**:
- We don't provide personal predictions
- We don't endorse astrology as science
- We don't connect planetary positions to individual destiny

**No Mixing Categories**:
- We don't use modern physics terminology to retrofit ancient narratives
- We don't claim quantum mechanics validates mythology
- We don't present symbolic language as literal physical description

## Why This Matters

**Intellectual Honesty**: Science and mythology serve different purposes. Blurring them diminishes both.

**Educational Responsibility**: Users deserve clear information about what is evidence-based versus what is traditional belief.

**Cultural Respect**: Traditional narratives have value as cultural heritage without needing to be "scientific."

**Safety**: False claims can mislead users and damage trust in actual science.

## What We Do Instead

**For Science**: Present evidence-based knowledge with proper sources and acknowledged limitations.

**For Narratives**: Present cultural stories as meaningful traditions that reflect values, history, and philosophical thought.

**For Connections**: When historical astronomical observations are encoded in traditional terminology, we explain both the observational basis and the narrative framework without conflating them.

## Language Guidelines

Avoid:
- "Ancient texts prove..."
- "This mythology predicted modern science..."
- "Spiritual energy aligns with quantum..."

Use:
- "This narrative explains..."
- "Traditional frameworks described..."
- "Cultural interpretations included..."
- "Modern science shows..."
- "Evidence indicates..."
